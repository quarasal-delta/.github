# Delta Studio

> *Creating Worlds Worth Remembering.*

Delta Studio is the game development division of the Quarasal ecosystem, dedicated to creating original 3D narrative-driven RPG experiences.

Our long-term vision is to build immersive fictional universes where every game tells a complete story, delivers meaningful gameplay, and leaves a lasting emotional impact. Rather than focusing on live-service models, we aim to create high-quality standalone titles that players can enjoy from beginning to end.

## ✪ 10-Year Strategic Vision

Delta Studio is currently building the foundation for a long-term game development ecosystem. Over the next decade, our strategy is divided into four stages.

### Phase 1 — Foundation (2026–2028)

- Build the core development team.
- Establish the Delta Studio brand.
- Develop internal pipelines and production workflows.
- Research Unreal Engine technologies and game development.
- Begin development of the first original IP.

---

### Phase 2 — First Original Title (2029–2031)

- Release the first standalone narrative RPG.
- Build a passionate player community.
- Improve development tools and production quality.
- Expand the studio with specialized talent.

---

### Phase 3 — Studio Expansion (2032–2034)

- Develop multiple original intellectual properties (IPs).
- Publish games across PC and console platforms.
- Strengthen art direction, storytelling, and cinematic production.
- Collaborate with international partners and publishers.

---

### Phase 4 — Long-Term Universe (2035–2036)

- Establish Delta as a recognized independent RPG studio.
- Build interconnected fictional universes through standalone titles.
- Expand into animation, novels, merchandise, and other creative media.
- Continue creating unforgettable stories for players around the world.

## ✪ Development Philosophy

We believe great RPGs should feel like complete works of art.

Every title is designed to provide a full, self-contained experience where players can immerse themselves in a meaningful story from beginning to end.

Rather than endless content updates, we focus on delivering memorable adventures that remain meaningful long after completion.
